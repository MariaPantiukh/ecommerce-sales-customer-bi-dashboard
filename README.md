# E-commerce BI Dashboard Ecosystem: Sales & Customer Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on building a complete Business Intelligence (BI) reporting system for a real E-commerce dropshipping business (operating since 2018 across Website, Instagram, and TikTok). 

The main goal was to transform raw CRM data into an automated visual ecosystem to drive data-backed decisions, optimize sales funnels, and improve customer retention.

**🔗 Interactive Dashboard Link:** [Looker Studio Report](https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/383c485b-5c8d-4417-9767-ecf6405e5d3c)

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
* **Data Source:** CRM system (E-commerce sales & customer transaction logs)
* **Data Preparation & Cleansing:** Google Sheets
* **Data Visualization & BI:** Looker Studio (Google Data Studio) 

---

## 🧼 Data Cleaning & Transformation Pipeline
Before building the dashboards, a comprehensive data preprocessing phase was executed to fix critical data quality issues:
1.  **Handling Missing Values:** Cleaned incomplete customer contact records and unassigned order statuses.
2.  **Data De-duplication:** Identified and merged duplicate customer profiles caused by multi-channel sales (Web, Instagram, TikTok).
3.  **Data Standardization:** Uniformed date formats, normalized product names, and aligned financial metrics (revenue, shipping costs, net profit).
4.  **Schema Alignment:** Prepared structured clean tables ready for efficient Looker Studio integration.

## 📊 Dashboard Architecture
The analytical solution is divided into two comprehensive interactive dashboards:

### 1. Sales Analytics Dashboard
* **Objective:** Track business health, sales dynamics, and channel performance.
* **Key Metrics:** Total Revenue, Net Profit, Order Count, Conversion Rate.
* **Core Visuals:** * Sales performance trends over time (MoM, YoY).
    * Top-performing product categories.

## 💡 Business Insights & Impact
Based on the finalized Looker Studio visuals, several key business insights were identified:
* **Inventory Optimization:** Highlighted seasonal peaks for key product categories to better manage supplier stock.
* **Targeted Ad Optimization:** Identified top-performing geographical regions and customer locations to significantly improve ad targeting and budget efficiency.
* **Product Portfolio Strategy:** Formed a "Top Products" matrix by analyzing the correlation between product popularity (order volume) and Average Order Value (AOV) to focus on high-margin winners.
